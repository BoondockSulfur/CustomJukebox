Place your .ogg music files here!

Files should be named exactly as defined in sounds.json, for example:
- epic_journey.ogg
- ocean_dreams.ogg
- forest_walk.ogg

To convert music to .ogg format:
1. Use Audacity (free): File → Export → Export as OGG
2. Or use ffmpeg: ffmpeg -i input.mp3 -c:a libvorbis -q:a 5 output.ogg
3. Or use online converter: cloudconvert.com

Recommendations:
- Sample rate: 44100 Hz
- Bitrate: 128-192 kbps
- Mono or Stereo both work
- Keep files under 10MB for best performance

See README.md in the parent folder for full instructions!
